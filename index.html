When you help individuals get better, we become better as a whole.

SafePoint is a mobile app that reduces HIV and Hepatitis C transmission in the injection drug use community by connecting users to the tools and resources they need to make better decisions.

What is SafePoint?

What We Do

We make it easier for injection drug users to get information on safer injection practices and harm reduction, clean injection equipment, and treatment when they are ready for help.

What We Believe

Anonymity, harm reduction, and community

Mobile App Features

Find Needle Exchange Programs

Search for nearby needle exchange programs. Find their hours, contact information, and directions from you to them.

Access Reliable Information

Access to curated, evolving and reliable information and tutorials for safer injection practices in the form of written text and informative videos that focus on preventing blood-borne pathogens infections and overdoses.

Get Help

Get help when your ready. SafePoint provides access to medical centres, counselling, therapy, and treatment facilities by finding nearby locations and providing contact information and directions.
